MVR - Patch Readerv0.92

MVR (My Virtual Rig) is an open-file-format container that packages the GDTF files for all of the lighting fixtures in your design, along with a complete 3D model and data describing the positions and 3D elements. (source: Vectorworks)

This web-application allow you to very quickly read its Fixture Patch. Everything is local, nothing is uploaded.
